Treatments

SHODANAM SHAMANA RASAYANAM DETOX

SHODANAM – For Severe Manifestations (Psoriasis Condition)

For individuals experiencing severe manifestations of psoriasis, including intense itching, burning, substantial flakes shedding from lesions, tingling sensations, stress-related factors, insomnia (due to disrupted sleep caused by itching), and lesions spreading over 50% of the total body surface, our SHODANAM treatment is tailored to address these severe symptoms.

SHAMANA – For Mild to Moderate Manifestations (Psoriasis Condition)

Designed for those with mild to moderate manifestations of psoriasis, our SHAMANA treatment addresses symptoms such as intermittent itching, irritation, dryness of lesions, loss of appetite, redness, and flaking.
